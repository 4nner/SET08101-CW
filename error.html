We're Sorry!

Something went wrong with your request.
This is what happened:

- There are not enough questions on the database for the quiz you tried to build.
- You've answered all the questions available in the database for the quiz you've just tried to build.


We will leave you the choice on how to solve this issue.
These are the two possible solutions:

- Build a quiz with different settings. This will give you a brand new quiz.
- Reset your session token. The website will forget what questions has served you, and you will be able to keep playing with the same setting.

Please be aware that by choosing to reset your token, you will play again with questions you have already answered to.
If after resetting the token you still get redirected to this page, it means that there are not enough questions in that category.
